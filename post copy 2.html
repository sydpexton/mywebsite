Dinner Recipes

White Chicken Chili Pie with Pumpkin Cornbread Crust

Such a fun cast iron skillet meal! Packed with tons of fun different ingredients providing your body with lots of nutrients it craves and needs!

This recipe calls for chicken broth or water. I recommend using a type of bone broth for added health benefits!

Nutritional benefits of bone broth:

  • Rich in minerals such as magnesium, calcium and phosphorus
  • Anti-inflammatory properties
  • Richest source of natural collagen
    • Collagen is like a glue that holds your body together. Vital for function especially for health of joints, skin and cartilage.
  • Contains gelatin
    • Gelatin allows the bones to move without friction and contributes to maintanence of strong and healthy bones.
    • Gelatin also feeds good bacteria in the gut (probiotics) and strengthens the lining of the gut.

Kale, White Bean and Orzo Soup

I love making a large batch of this cozy soup so that I can eat it for meals throughout the week!

Nutritional benefits:

  • Kale
    • Fiber
    • Rich in vitamins and minerals such as vitamin A, K and Calcium
    • Anti-inflammatory
    • Antioxidants - protects cells from damage
    • Helps detoxification at cellular level
  • White Beans
    • Plant based protein
  • Orzo
    • Can be a source of healthy whole grain but make sure the ingredients use whole wheat and organic if possible

Cashew Chicken Casserole

Casseroles are an easy way to meal prep. Just prepare the ingredients, throw it in a baking dish, let it bake, and then it's all done! They are also a great way to combine lots of ingredients to get your veggies, whole grains, fiber, and protein all in one meal!

Nutritional benefits of cashews (one of the healthiest nuts!):

  • Support heart and brain health
  • Helps with digestion and absorption of nutrients
  • Source of healthy fats
  • Plant based protein
  • Rich in magnesium and therefore supports the bones
