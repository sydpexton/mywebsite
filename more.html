Recipe of the week:

Homemade Granola Bars

  • Ingredients
    • 2 c nuts (you can cut some of the softer nuts up in smaller chunks so the bars stick together better)
      • Almonds
      • Cashews
      • Walnuts
      • Sunflower Seeds
      • Pepitas
    • 1/2 c coconut flakes
    • 1/4 c goji berries (or other dried fruit)
    • 1 tbsp chia seeds
    • 1/4 tsp salt
    • 1/4 c maple syrup
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Steps
    1. Mix nuts and seeds together
    2. Pour maple syrup and vanilla over nuts. Stir until everything is well coated.
    3. Put mixture into a parchment lined 8x8 dish
    4. Bake for 30-35 minutes at 325 degrees farenheight
    5. Let it cool for an hour before cutting into bars
