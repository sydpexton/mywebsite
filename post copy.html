Lunch Recipes

Lentil Veggie Quesadilla

Simple lunch packed with veggies and fiber! I love using sprouted grain tortillas because they provide a variety of whole grains. These grains have had time to sprout and germinate before getting turned into flour so they have absorbed more nutrients. The combo of grains makes it a complete protein, meaning it has all 9 of the essential amino acids.

Nutritional benefits of lentils:

  • Rich in fiber and support digestive health
  • Plant based source of protein
  • High source of phytosterols which helps lower cholesterol
  • Helps reduce plaque and therefore prevent heart disease
  • Rich in phytochemicals - a chemical in plants that can reduce risk of cancer

Miso Sesame Noodles

Super easy recipe! This recipe calls for angel hair noodles but you can get creative with the type of noodles you use. I used edemame noodles for a boost of protein and magnesium.

Nutritional benefits of miso:

  • Made from fermented soybeans providing probiotic bacteria
    • Probiotics add healthy bacteria to the gastrointestinal tract and therefore improves gut health
  • Anti-inflammatory properties
  • Better immune function
  • Contains nutrients like copper, phosphorus, manganese, and Vitamin K that help your body absorb other nutrients

Chicken Peanut Wraps

Great option for a fast lunch because you can leave the mixture of veggies and peanut sauce in a bowl and store in the fridge. Then when it is time to eat all you need to do is grab your tortila and fill it with the mixture!

Nutritional benefits:

  • Peanuts
    • Contain more protein than any other nut!
  • Chicken
    • Source of omega-6 fatty acid
    • Source of lean protein
